Paint, Sip & New Friends in Ikoyi 🎨🍷

What if making new friends was as easy as picking up a brush and taking a sip?

Join us for a relaxed and creative paint and sip experience in Ikoyi. This beginner friendly session is designed for people who want to unwind, try something new, and meet interesting people in a warm and social environment.

Start with a Welcome Reception

We begin the evening with a 20 minute welcome reception where everyone can settle in, grab a drink, and start chatting. It is an easy, low pressure way to break the ice before the painting begins.

A Guided Paint & Sip Experience

You will then enjoy a 2 hour guided painting session led by a professional instructor. No experience is needed. You will be guided step by step to create your own artwork while enjoying drinks throughout the session.

Painting is naturally social. It gives you something to focus on while conversations flow easily around you, making it effortless to connect with new people.

Meet People You Are Likely to Click With

The event runs in small, thoughtfully matched groups of up to 20 people, based on age range and vibe so you are surrounded by people you are more likely to connect with.

Before the event, you will be added to a private group chat with your group. Each chat includes a friendly host who helps break the ice, spark conversations, and get everyone comfortable before meeting in person.

Many groups use the chat to plan drinks, dinner, or a hangout before or after the session, often turning the experience into a full social evening.

Designed to Turn a Fun Evening Into Real Friendships

The group chat stays open for two weeks after the event, giving everyone time to keep the conversation going and plan another meetup.

Every time the group meets again, the chat automatically extends for another two weeks. This way, what starts as a fun night of painting can grow into real, lasting friendships.

At the end of the session, you will take home your finished painting, a personal reminder of a creative night filled with new connections.

Event Overview

  • Welcome reception
  • 2 hour guided paint and sip session with instructor
  • Drinks included throughout the experience
  • Carefully matched groups of up to 20 people
  • Private group chat with a host before the event
  • Chat stays open two weeks after the event to plan meetups
  • Total experience: about 2.5 hours

Come solo, bring your curiosity, and leave with your own artwork and maybe a few new friends.

Spots are limited to keep the experience intimate. 🎨✨
